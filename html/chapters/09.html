Format Type Description
MP4 Video A widely used format for high-quality video compression. Compatible with most devices and platforms.
MKV Video A flexible and open-source format that supports high-definition video and multiple audio tracks.
AVI Video An older video format that offers good quality but tends to have larger file sizes.
MP3 Audio A popular audio format known for its good balance of sound quality and file size.
WAV Audio A high-quality, uncompressed audio format commonly used in professional settings.
FLAC Audio A lossless audio format that retains original sound quality while reducing file size.
